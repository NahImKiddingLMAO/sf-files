Welcome to Secret Fun!

Secret Fun was originally created by Ericsson Ansah-Antwi on December 8th, 2020. It was created to be a haven for students to find all of the best unblocked games. This website is created in partnership with the original Google Document to bring Secret Fun to the general masses, and to every school district!
